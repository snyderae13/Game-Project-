WIZARD DEFENDER

Welcome to Wizard Defender!

This game is about defending the Wizard Kingdom from the Dragon Menace!

Instructions:

Place cusor over any space in the game screen to place your Wizard.

Each Wizard costs 100 resource points.

Your fireball will damage the dragons by 20.

After each dragon is killed, you will receive 20 points added to your resources.
